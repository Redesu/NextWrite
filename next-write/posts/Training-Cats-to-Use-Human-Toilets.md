### Phase Overview  
| Week | Task | Success Rate |  
|------|------|-------------|  
| 1 | Move litter box beside toilet | 92% |  
| 2 | Elevate box with phone books | 87% |  
| 3 | Transition to training seat | 64% |  

Warning: Persian cats often refuse due to short legs.
